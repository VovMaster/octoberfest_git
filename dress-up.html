STYLE IT UP WITH A WIESN GLUPPERL

The most unpronounceable piece of Oktoberfest traditional outfitting is a simple name badge. The Wiesn Glupperl – say it like you read it & try not to put any e’s where they’re not written – is a simple wooden pin with your name engraved in it.
