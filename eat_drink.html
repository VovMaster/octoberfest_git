EAT & DRINK

BAVARIAN FOOD
TRUCK VILLAGE

Germans are known for their amazing food. Explore our food truck village where you’ll find mouth-watering German delights to feast upon including Schweinshaxe (pork knuckle), Bratwurst, Schnitzel, Pretzels, Strudel and of course some Aussie favourites too!

INCLUDING:

Berlin Bangers:
Working with their master German butcher, they’ve carefully crafted the finest sausage in our land. Free from preservatives, gluten, dairy and fillers. They’ve even got a vegan Bratwurst!

Rolling Schnitzel:
Cook to order gourmet schnitzel food truck making wraps, burgers, and snack packs with the tasty core ingredient. Great food, fast and with careful consideration to each ingredient.

Alpenhütte:
Stoked to share one of Bavaria’s most traditional and beloved food gems with you. Kasespätzle is an irresistibly-tasty melted cheese dish. Spätzle-pasta, caramelized onions, chives, and freshly ground fresh pepper will be an unforgettable food experience.

& SO MUCH MORE

CRAFT BEER HALL

One of the most important (and traditional) attractions of Oktoberfest Parklands is the ‘liquid gold’. Embracing the spirit of Munich, we’re bringing the beer hall experience direct to Canberra. From the hand-selected range of the very best German (& a few non-German) beers to the brass and Oompah and the long tables, the friendships and festivities, it’s time to embrace your inner German!

For those who prefer something other than beer, naturally our bars are stocked with quality drinks of all sorts.

12-PM – 10PM Sat 14 October
Parliamentary Triangle, Canberra
