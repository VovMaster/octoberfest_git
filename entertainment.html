ENTERTAINMENT

OOMPAH BANDS

It wouldn’t be Oktoberfest without German music, and Oompah bands are the soundtrack to your Oktoberfest experience! These big brass bands are creative, interactive and energetic. Even without speaking any German you’ll feel like you know the words and find yourself singing along to traditional Oktoberfest anthems:

‘Ein Prosit’
‘Fliegerlied (The Flying Song)’

as well as modern classics like
‘I Love Rock ‘n Roll’
‘Highway To Hell’
‘Seven Nation Army’
‘We Are The Champions’
