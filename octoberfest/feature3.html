BEER KELLNER FAQ

WHEN ARE THE BEER KELLNER AVAILABLE TO BOOK?

Beer kellner are available during Oktoberfest Parklands, from midday 14 October until 8pm (2 hours before close).

HOW LONG CAN I BOOK A BEER KELLNER FOR?

You can book a beer kellner in blocks of an hour and until 8pm. If you have more than 5 people we recommend that you book a minimum of 2 Beer Kellner to get the full experience.

WHO ARE THE BEER KELLNER?

The Beer Kellner are a collection of experienced hospitality staff who are local to Canberra. They’ve been selected based on their service level, banter skills and all have their current RSA.

THERE AREN’T ANY BEER KELLNER SHOWING UP AS AVAILABLE, HOW CAN I BOOK ONE?

If you can’t see any kellner available online, it means we are booked out for that day. You can try another night, or join our waitlist by sending us an email at gretl@oktoberfestparklands.com.au. If a cancellation pops up, we’ll be in touch.

WHAT EXACTLY CAN A BEER KELLNER DO?

Besides wait on you hand and foot? Here’s what beer kellner can do for you;

  • Order food & beverages on your behalf without having to wait in line*
  • Reserve a table for you*
  • Curate your experience by creating a menu for the night for your group
  • Deliver your food and beverages
  • Be your personal photographer
  • Give you a tour of the event, let you know what’s popular, their recommendations and guide you to where everything is located

* Only available to The Beer kellner and their guests

I HAVE TO CANCEL MY RESERVATION – HOW CAN I DO THIS?

You can cancel your reservation by contacting us here. We’ll remove it from our system and process the refund (pending our cancellation policy)

WHAT IS THE CANCELLATION POLICY?

  • 100% of your payment will be returned if cancelled 5 days or more from date
  • 70% of your payment will be returned if cancelled 4 days from date
  • 50% of your payment will be returned if 48-hour cancellation
  • 0% returned if your booking is cancelled within 24 hours

WHAT CAN’T THE BEER KELLNER DO?

There is only a small list of what beer Kellner can’t do;

  • Make the chefs cook faster
  • Hand feed you
  • Park or drive your vehicle
  • Take responsibility for any of your valuables

WHEN DO I PAY FOR THE BEER KELLNER?

You are required to pay for your beer kellner in full at the time of booking. We take most major credit cards and it is managed all through our booking system Checkfront

HOW DO I KNOW WHERE TO MEET MY BEER KELLNER?

48hrs before your reservation you’ll be sent an email from Gretl outlining the details of where to meet your beer kellner at Oktoberfest Parklands. If you don’t hear from her, you can always get in touch here.
