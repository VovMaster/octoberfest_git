TICKET FAQ

HOW TO ACCESS PRE-SALE TICKETS?

Limited Pre-sale tickets will only be available to oktoberfestparklands.com.au subscribers and only available from midday 14 August until allocation exhausted. Sign up by 9pm 13 August for your chance to get pre-sale tickets.

DOES +BF MEAN I CAN BRING MY BEST FRIEND FOR FREE?

Leider nein. The ‘bf’ after the ticket price means ‘plus booking fee’, not ‘plus best friend’.

WHAT’S INCLUDED IN MY TICKET?

Entry, wunderbar entertainment, a commemorative Oktoberfest Parklands Beer Stein (beer glass) and your first drink is on us.

DOES MY NAME HAVE TO BE ON THE TICKET?

No, you just need to bring your valid ticket and ensure that no one else has used it already to gain entry. Treat your ticket like cash! Don't leave it lying around to be copied.

I AM UNDER 18 YEARS OF AGE, CAN I COME TO OKTOBERFEST?

Nein, Oktoberfest Parklands is a strictly 18+ event. All ticket holders will be asked to provide valid proof of ID to gain entry on the day (current Australian driver’s license, passport or Australian government issued proof of age card).

I AM OVER 18 BUT DON’T HAVE A PHOTO ID, BUT I TOTALLY LOOK OVER 21. WILL I BE ALLOWED IN?

Nein. As Oktoberfest Parklands is a strictly 18+ event, each ticket holder will have their ID checked upon arrival. You need to hold a valid photo ID for entry or you will be denied.

DO I HAVE TO PRINT MY TICKET OR CAN I BRING IT ON MY PHONE?

Both methods will work at Oktoberfest Parklands (so long as your screen isn’t cracked into oblivion).
