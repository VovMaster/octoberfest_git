EVENT CONDITIONS OF ENTRY

  1. Oktoberfest Parklands is strictly an 18+ event. Bring your ID and your e-ticket to gain entry into the event. You MUST have valid photo ID to get into the event, such as a current Australian driver’s license (international driver’s licenses won’t be accepted), State Approved ‘Proof Of Age’ card or a valid Passport.
  2. Upon entry, your ticket will be exchanged for a wristband, which you MUST keep on for the duration of the event. Lost wristbands will not be replaced.
  3. Entry is at own risk. The right to refuse entry is reserved.
  4. The Promoter and the Venue shall not be held liable for any loss, injury or damages sustained entering or within the premises.
  5. Security staff are there to look after you and are entitled to search, pat down or metal detect any patron wishing to enter the event or inside the event. Patrons failing to comply will be refused entry / ejected from the event without refund.
  6. Oktoberfest Parklands reserves the right to refuse entry to ticket holders who are clearly under the influence of drugs or alcohol or illustrate behaviour that intends to disturb public peace or provoke assault, hatred or rage.
  7. This event has a strict NO PASSOUTS policy which means, once you are inside the venue and decide to leave, you will not be able to re-enter the venue.
  8. There will be an express entry lane for people without bags. It is wise to bring only essential items for the day.
  9. Oktoberfest Parklands will rock out, rain or shine.
  10. Small still cameras (including those with a recording function) and mobile phones are permitted for personal use only. Professional still cameras (such as an SLR), professional video cameras (including go-pros), sound recording equipment and professional lighting and tripod set ups are not permitted at Oktoberfest Parklands without written authority from the Promoters.
  11. The Promoter reserves the right to change the line-up and/or the date of the event without prior notification. In the event of a change of the date or lineup, refunds will not be available.
  12. Police will be present at the event. Drug detection dogs will also be in attendance.
  13. Police will be conducting roadside breath-testing and drug-testing on nearby roads.
  14. Make sure you know what you cannot bring into the event – check the Banned Items list
  15. The promoter reserves the right to refuse entry to any person wearing motorcycle colours, patches or insignia.
  16. Bring the essentials - plenty of protective clothing, a hat and sunglasses, or a raincoat if weather indicates.
  17. The use of earplugs is recommended if long term hearing loss is of concern to you. The Promoters of the event will not be held liable for any hearing damage or loss.
  18. Avoid dehydration - don't forget to drink water.
  19. You should bring adequate cash for the whole day, as there are very limited cash or credit facilities on site.
  20. Look out for your friends and fellow punters, fast responses save lives. First aid facilities will be available in several locations around the site. Go to those areas for assistance. Security are also there to assist
  21. You acknowledge that photos and video footage may be taken on the event site and agree release, to the promoter, any photos or video footage taken of you while on or entering the event site for use in promotional material
  22. DANGEROUS ACTIVITIES WILL NOT BE TOLERATED. This includes aggressive moshing, stage diving, crowd surfing and climbing. Patrons participating in these dangerous activities will be evicted without right to re-entry or refund. The Promoter accepts no liability for any injuries or damages caused by these activities.
  23. If at any time you feel your safety or anyone else’s safety is threatened, please contact the nearest security guard.
  24. In the case of an emergency evacuation or situation, you must follow all official directives.
  25. To keep Oktoberfest Parklands a clean and green event, the organisers encourage all patrons to utilise the recycling and refuse facilities provided throughout the site. It's your site: keep it clean!!
  26. Please don’t drop ciggie butts on the ground. BIN IT! Help keep the event litter free!
  27. Lost property will be held as a courtesy for two days (post-event) only, after which it will be handed to local police. No responsibility is accepted by the Promoter for property lost at Oktoberfest Parklands.
